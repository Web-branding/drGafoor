About Our Medical Center

In the year 1997, Dr. Abdul Gafoor P in association with ITALIAN HERBAL CENTRE started his medical practice in Ajman, UAE. We follow an integrated alternative medical system combining Ayurveda, Homeopathy, Acupuncture and Yoga.

Ayurveda is not just a system of treating illness, it is a science of life. It offers a body of wisdom designed to help people stay vital while realizing their full human potential. Ayurveda maintains a balance between the interrelated relationships within the body and mind.

Homeopathy is a branch of alternative medicine originated in Germany based on the principle "like cures like".
