Shirodhara is said to have relaxing, soothing, and calming effects on the body and mind. Shirodhara is good for various skin diseases, sleeplessness, facial palsy, nervous disorders stress and insanity.

Udwarthanam

Udwarthanam is special therapeutic deep tissue massage using herbal powders. This massage is done so as to stimulate hair follicles and subcutaneous fat tissues. Highly effective in reducing obesity and refershes the body.

Kativasti

Kativasti is a special type of low back therapy or lumbar therapy. The word Kati stands for low back and Vasti for containing or retaining something inside. It is a rejuvenation therapy using hot oil or herbal decoctions.

Abhyangam

Abhyangam is performed over a specially designed wooden table (Droni) by trained masseurs. The sesame oil processed with various herbs is selected based on persons doshic type. It is relaxing and refreshing full body massage.

Nasya

Tharpanam

Netra Tharpanam is a rejuvenating treatment for our eyes. For strengthening the optical nerves, prevent cataract and provide a cooling effect, pure and medicated ghee is applied on the eyes. Usefull for people working on computers and mobiles.

Marmachikitsa

Marma means the most vulnerable spots in our body. It is the most powerful treatment in ayurveda providing an overall healing experience. It uses neuromuscular and orthopedic science. So it is very effective in treating dislocated discs, shoulder, angle etc.

This is a type of treatment with medicines known as bhasmas. It is very effective in curing acute stages of various diseases. Bhasma are very fine ayurvedic medicinal powders prepared by the process of calcination of metals, minerals and natural stones or gem.
